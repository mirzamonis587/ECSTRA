Detectron

Smarter detection for safer hospitals.

Our Pathogen Detection Device is a portable hardware solution that enables direct detection of microbes on hospital surfaces using a 1000x portable microlens. It integrates NVIDIA Jetson Nano's AI/ML capabilities for real-time image processing, providing instant feedback without the need for swab sampling. This device enhances hospital hygiene by offering quick, non-destructive and accurate surface monitoring to reduce the risk of hospital-acquired infections (HAIs).

Our advanced pathogen detection device combines high-resolution imaging with AI analysis to detect and identify microbes on hospital surfaces in real-time. Equipped with a microlens, LED array, and a front-facing display, it ensures fast, accurate, and efficient contamination assessment.

working Process

01

Surface illumination

The LED array illuminates the surface to be analyzed

02

Image Capture and Magnification

The digital microlens (1000x magnification) captures and magnifies the surface..

03

Preprocessing and AI/ML Inference

The captured image is sent to the AI/ML microcontroller. The device applies image preprocessing (e.g., contrast enhancement, noise reduction).

04

Result Visualization

The LCD display shows real time magnified image and the respective output

05

Data Storage and Connectivity

Results and images are stored locally on the SD card or sent via Wi-Fi/Bluetooth to a central server or cloud for further analysis.
