Incubation Program
at ECSTRA

A launchpad for startups to grow with expert guidance, funding support and collaborative space.

Empowering Early-Stage Healthcare Innovation

This program is purpose-built to empower early-stage healthcare startups—specifically those that have moved beyond ideation and have developed a validated concept, prototype, or proof of concept (PoC). These innovators often face a critical bottleneck: while their ideas show promise, they lack the structured support, strategic guidance, and ecosystem access needed to evolve into market-ready ventures.

The incubation program addresses this "valley of death"—the high-risk phase between validation and commercialization—by providing a curated blend of technical, clinical, and business support designed specifically for healthcare ventures.

Program Structure

Structured mentorship and training to shape early-stage healthcare ideas.

Orientation & Foundation Development

4th Nov. 2024 - 9th Nov. 2024

This phase familiarizes participants with the program, builds awareness of the healthcare ecosystem, and lays the groundwork for idea development through expert sessions and foundational training.

Clinical Emersion

11th Nov. 2024 - 23rd Nov. 2024

The Clinical Immersion phase involves hospital visits where participants observe real-time processes to identify pain points and challenges in the healthcare system.

POC & Business Model Development

25th Nov. 2024 - 14th Dec. 2024

In this phase, participants develop a Proof of Concept (PoC) for their solution and work on building a basic business model. They explore value proposition, target users and market fit to shape a viable path for their innovation.
