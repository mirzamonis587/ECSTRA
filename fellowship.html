Fellowship/Internship Program
at ECSTRA

Who are we looking for?

We are looking for talented individuals from a wide array of disciplines like engineering, design, business and the medical field who are looking to innovate. We can also offer a position as a working student or for writing a bachelor or master thesis or Ph.D. thesis..

What will be your tasks at ECSTRA?

By working with ECSTRA you will have a chance to interact with a global team every day and gain hands on experience to a wide variety of healthcare related projects starting day one of your fellowship!

What will you get?

  • Work on real healthcare challenges with clinical and technical teams.
  • Enhance technical, research, and soft skills with expert mentorship.
  • Receive a certificate, stipend, and letter of recommendation.
  • Network opportunity with professionals across disciplines.

Innovate to Elevate

Internships provide a platform to apply knowledge in real-world settings. At ECSTRA, we empower interns to think critically and innovate boldly. Together, we cultivate the next generation of problem-solvers.
