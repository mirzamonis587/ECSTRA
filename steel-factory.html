Steel Fabrication Unit

Precision-crafted steel solutions for healthcare, education and institutional excellence.

Strong Foundations for Smarter Spaces

Era University houses a dedicated Steel Fabrication Unit that designs and manufactures high-quality, durable metal products tailored for both hospital infrastructure and academic environments. With a focus on precision engineering, safety and longevity, the unit supports in-house demands while contributing to institutional self-reliance and cost efficiency. The unit is equipped with advanced cutting, welding, grinding, drilling, and powder-coating machinery.
All products meet industry safety and hygiene standards, with stainless steel and powder-coated options to ensure durability in clinical settings. The unit is ISO-certified and adheres to hospital-grade fabrication protocols, ensuring every item is built for long-term functionality and ease of maintenance.

Hospital Furniture

The steel unit manufactures high-quality hospital furniture including manual and semi-electric patient beds, overbed tables, bedside lockers, examination tables, IV stands and doctor's stools. Each item is designed for durability, hygiene and patient comfort.

Institutional & Office Furnishings

From steel almirahs to lab and office tables, modular workstations and filing cabinets, the unit supports administrative and academic spaces with robust and practical furniture solutions tailored for daily use.
