Era’s Center for Start-Up Training, Research and Acceleration(ECSTRA)

"We are a healthcare innovation hub at Era University, bringing together innovators, experts and healthcare professionals to turn impactful ideas into real-world solutions. Through collaborative partnerships, expert mentorship and access to state-of-the-art resources, we support the development of technologies and strategies that address critical healthcare challenges."

Vision

Our vision is to become a leading center in India for healthcare innovation, turning ideas into real solutions that make a big impact.

Mission

Our goal is to drive healthcare innovation by bringing together different fields, using advanced technology, and focusing on research that solves real problems.

Objectives

We aim to support the creation of next-generation healthcare solutions by offering expert guidance, modern facilities, and a strong support system for innovators.

About Founder

Mr. Zaw Ali Khan is the Founder & CEO of Criterion Tech and the visionary behind ECSTRA at Era University. With a strong focus on healthcare innovation, he has led the development of smart ICU systems, telemedicine platforms, and simulation tools. Through ECSTRA, he fosters healthcare entrepreneurship by bridging clinical insight with technology and mentorship. Recognized for his work in digital health, he has spoken at major forums like the Global Digital Health Summit and received the Asia Award for Best Quality Initiative in Healthcare. He also leads True Form Games, creating immersive tools for medical education, and holds multiple directorships in healthcare and education ventures.
