Pre-Incubation Programs
at ECSTRA

A structured program to nurture early-stage ideas through mentoring, validation, and prototype development.

Laying the Foundation for Startup Success.

ECSTRA’s Pre-Incubation Program is specifically designed to support individuals with early-stage, unrefined ideas in the healthcare sector. Whether you're a student with a concept, a researcher or a first-time innovator passionate about solving real-world healthcare challenges, this program provides the structured foundation you need to begin your entrepreneurial journey.

By the end of the program, participants will have developed a clearly articulated problem statement, validated solution approach, initial business model and in many cases, a working prototype or mock-up.

Program Structure

Structured mentorship and training to shape early-stage healthcare ideas.

Orientation & Foundation Development

4th Nov. 2024 - 9th Nov. 2024

This phase familiarizes participants with the program, builds awareness of the healthcare ecosystem, and lays the groundwork for idea development through expert sessions and foundational training.

Clinical Emersion / Hospital Visit

11th Nov. 2024 - 23rd Nov. 2024

The Clinical Immersion phase involves hospital visits where participants observe real-time processes to identify pain points and challenges in the healthcare system.

POC & Business Model Development

25th Nov. 2024 - 14th Dec. 2024

In this phase, participants develop a Proof of Concept (PoC) for their solution and work on building a basic business model. They explore value proposition, target users and market fit to shape a viable path for their innovation.

Benifits

Access to hands-on workshops on ideation, problem validation, and solution mapping

Support in creating business model canvases, customer personas, and stakeholder analysis

Participation in pitch rounds with feedback from jury
