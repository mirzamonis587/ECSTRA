Empowering healthcare through skill and service.

Era’s Institute of Allied Health Sciences & Research is a dynamic and well-integrated part of Era University, offering diverse educational pathways in paramedical and allied health fields—from diplomas to doctoral programs. Embedded in a university known for its research-leaning, multidisciplinary approach, the institute equips compassionate professionals with robust academic foundations and real-world clinical experience.

Diploma Programs

Gain professional healthcare skills with our 1-2 year diploma programs for a fast-track career.

Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology

Trains students in laboratory techniques for analyzing blood, tissues and body fluids, supporting accurate medical diagnoses.

Diploma in X-Ray Technician

Prepares students to operate X-ray equipment and perform diagnostic imaging procedures, assisting doctors in accurate diagnosis and patient care.

Diploma in Operation Theatre Technician

Prepares students to assist in surgical procedures by maintaining sterile environments, handling instruments, and supporting surgeons in the operation theatre.

Diploma in MRI Technician

Trains students to operate MRI scanners and produce detailed images, supporting doctors in diagnosing internal conditions safely and accurately.

Diploma in Anesthesia & Critical Care

Equips students with skills to assist anesthesiologists and manage patients in critical care settings, ensuring safe anesthesia delivery and emergency support during surgeries.

Diploma in Emergency & Trauma Care

Trains students to provide immediate medical assistance in emergencies and trauma situations, supporting doctors in critical care and life-saving interventions.

Diploma in Respiratory Therapy Technician

Focuses on microorganisms and their role in human disease, infection control and laboratory diagnostics.

Bachelor's Programs

Shape your future in healthcare through 3 to 4 years of specialized study.

B.Sc. in Radiological Imaging Techniques

Equips students with skills to operate advanced imaging equipment, perform diagnostic radiological procedures, and assist healthcare teams in accurate patient diagnosis.
Duration: 3 years.

B.Sc. in Medical Lab Technology

Provides comprehensive training in laboratory techniques and diagnostics, enabling students to analyze samples and support disease detection in healthcare settings.
Duration: 3 years.

Bachelor of Optometry

Provides in-depth training in eye care, vision testing, and the management of visual disorders, preparing students for careers as optometrists in clinical and community settings.
Duration: 4 years.

Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT)

Provides comprehensive education in physical therapy, rehabilitation, and patient mobility, preparing students to help restore movement and function after injury or illness.
Duration: 4.5 years.

Master's Programs

Deepen your knowledge and skills through 2 years of focused study.

M.Sc. in Physiotherapy (with specializations like Neuro, Ortho, Cardio-Pulmonary)

This program offers advanced training in physiotherapy with options to specialize in neurology, orthopedics or cardio-pulmonary care. Graduates gain expertise in rehabilitation, patient assessment and evidence-based therapeutic techniques.
Duration: 2 years.

M.Sc. in Optometry

Prepares students to operate X-ray equipment and perform diagnostic imaging procedures, assisting doctors in accurate diagnosis and patient care.

M.Sc. in Medical Lab Techniques

This program provides advanced training in laboratory diagnostics, research methodologies, and quality control, preparing graduates for leadership roles in clinical laboratories and research settings.
Duration: 2 years.

Doctoral Programs

Pursue excellence with 3 to 5 years of groundbreaking research.

Ph.D. in Allied Health Sciences (various specializations)

This doctoral program enables candidates to conduct original research and contribute new knowledge in specialized areas of allied health sciences. Graduates are prepared for leadership roles in academia, research, and advanced clinical practice.
Duration: 3-5 years.
