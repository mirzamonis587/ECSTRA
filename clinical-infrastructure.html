Clinical Infrastructure
of ECSTRA

Era's Lucknow Medical College & Hospital has modern facilities like advanced labs, operation theatres, ICUs and emergency units. Along with this strong setup, it uses new technologies and research-based methods to improve patient care. By combining good infrastructure with innovative ideas, the hospital provides better treatment, faster recovery and a space for learning and medical research.
