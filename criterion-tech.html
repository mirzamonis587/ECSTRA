Pioneering Change in Healthcare Solutions

Criterion Tech is a company focused on developing innovative solutions for the healthcare and education sectors. It was founded in 2019. Zaw Ali Khan, the director of Criterion Tech, also serves as an additional director at Era's Lucknow Medical College and Hospital. This connection highlights a collaboration between the company and the university, particularly in the area of medical education and technology.

Innovative solutions

Experience the best of what Criterion Tech has innovated

Educational solutions

Criterion Tech Era provides immersive learning tools like Medico Skills, AR/VR training, and Edumation LMS, enabling healthcare students and professionals to gain hands-on experience in a virtual environment.

Health IT Solutions

Their IT suite includes systems like DigiDoctor HIS, Knowmed and Telemedicine portals that digitize hospital workflows, manage patient data efficiently and improve clinical decision-making.

Medical Innovations

With devices such as Pocket ECG, Smart Analyzers, and Ventilator Data Services, Criterion delivers next-gen diagnostic solutions for real-time, accurate patient monitoring and treatment.

Enhanced Productivity solutions

Solutions such as CornCall (employee support), biometric attendance and duty scheduling systems help hospitals reduce manual workload and optimize staff performance.

Homecare & Healthy Lifestyle Solutions.

Tools like DigiDoctor Telemedicine, NutriAnalyser and wearable ECGs support patients at home by promoting healthy habits and enabling remote health tracking.
