Era's College of Nursing

Era's College of Nursing trains skilled, compassionate nurses with strong academic and practical expertise for quality patient care.

click here to know more

Empowering healthcare through skill and service.

Era College of Nursing is dedicated to preparing compassionate and competent nursing professionals through high-quality education, practical training, and a strong focus on patient care. The college offers well-structured programs, modern facilities, and experienced faculty to ensure students gain both theoretical knowledge and clinical skills, enabling them to excel in the healthcare sector.
Era College of Nursing offers a degree course in Nursing. The duration of the B.Sc Nursing course is 4 years and Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing is 2 years. Candidate in the age group of 17 years to 35 years are eligible for admission

Diploma Programs

Gain professional healthcare skills with our diploma programs for a fast-track career.

General Nursing and Midwifery (G.N.M.)

This program prepares students to provide comprehensive nursing care and midwifery services in hospitals and community settings.
Duration: 3-3.5 years.

Auxiliary Nursing & Midwifery (A.N.M.)

This program trains students in basic nursing care, maternal and child health, and community healthcare services. Graduates are equipped to support healthcare teams in rural and urban settings.
Duration: 2 years.

Bachelor's Programs

Shape your future in healthcare through 3 to 4 years of specialized study.

B.Sc. Nursing

This program provides comprehensive education in nursing theory and clinical practice, preparing students to deliver quality patient care in various healthcare settings.
Duration: 4 years.

Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing

This program is designed for registered nurses to upgrade their skills and knowledge in advanced nursing practices, leadership, and patient care. It enables graduates to take on greater responsibilities in clinical and community healthcare settings.
Duration: 2 years.

Master's Programs

Deepen your knowledge and skills through 2 years of focused study.

M.Sc. Nursing (various specializations)

This program provides advanced knowledge and clinical skills in nursing, allowing students to specialize in areas such as medical-surgical, pediatric, psychiatric, or community health nursing. Graduates are prepared for leadership, teaching, and advanced practice roles in healthcare.
Duration: 2 years.
