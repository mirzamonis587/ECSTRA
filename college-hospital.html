Excellence in Education, Care in Service.

Era Educational Trust (EET) was formally registered in 2001 with approval by the Medical Council of India and Government of India, aiming to build a centre of excellence in medical education, training and healthcare.
Era’s Lucknow Medical College & Hospital officially began operations between 1997 (founding of college) and 2001 (hospital establishment), under EET’s oversight.
Affiliated to Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia Avadh University until 2016, and thereafter to Era University (which was itself established in 2016 by EET).

Anatomy

Focuses on the study of human body structure through dissection, models, and advanced imaging techniques.

Biochemistry

Explores chemical processes within living organisms, supporting diagnostics and research in medicine.

Physiology

Studies the functions and mechanisms of the human body, providing a foundation for clinical medicine.

Pathology

Deals with the study and diagnosis of diseases through examination of tissues, organs, and bodily fluids.

Pharmacology

Studies drug actions, interactions, and effects, essential for safe and effective patient care.

Community Medicine

Promotes health and disease prevention at the community level through education, research, and outreach.

Microbiology

Focuses on microorganisms and their role in human disease, infection control, and laboratory diagnostics.

Forensic Medicine

Applies medical knowledge to legal issues, including autopsies and medico-legal investigations.

Obstetrics Gynaecology

Provides care for women’s reproductive health, pregnancy, childbirth, and related disorders.

Pediatrics

Specializes in the medical care of infants, children, and adolescents, ensuring healthy development.

General Medicine

Manages a wide range of adult diseases, focusing on diagnosis, treatment, and prevention.

Emergency Medicine

Delivers immediate care for acute illnesses and injuries, operating 24/7 for critical situations.

Pulmonary Medicine

Diagnoses and treats diseases of the respiratory system, including asthma, COPD, and infections.

Psychiatry

Focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of mental, emotional, and behavioral disorders.

Dermatology and Veneral Disease

Deals with skin, hair, nail disorders, and sexually transmitted infections.

General Surgery

Performs surgical procedures for a variety of conditions involving different organ systems.

Anaesthesiology

Provides anesthesia and pain management during surgeries and critical care procedures.

Orthopedic Surgery

Treats musculoskeletal system disorders, including bones, joints, ligaments, and muscles.

Opthalmology

Specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of eye diseases and vision care.

Radiodiagnosis

Uses imaging technologies like X-rays, CT, MRI, and ultrasound for diagnosis and treatment planning.

Dentistry

Provides oral health care, including prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of dental diseases.

Oto rhino Laryngology

Deals with disorders of the ear, nose, throat, head, and neck.

Medical Education Unit

Enhances teaching and learning methods for faculty and students, promoting academic excellence.

Super Speciality Departments

Pediatric Surgery

Biotechnology

Focuses on the diagnosis and surgical treatment of urinary tract and male reproductive system disorders.

Neurosurgery

Performs surgical procedures on the brain, spine, and nervous system for trauma, tumors, and other conditions.

Plastic Surgery

Offers reconstructive and cosmetic surgical procedures to restore form and function after injury or disease.
