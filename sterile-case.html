Sterile Case for IV bottles

Care in every drop

A sterile case is a clean, contamination-free packaging or container designed to protect IV fluids and normal saline bottles from microbial or physical contamination before use.

Know more about Sterile case

Core Functions

Features of Sterile Case

  • High-quality polycarbonate sheet
  • Only 760 gm - lightweight and easy to handle
  • Resistant to impact and sterilization processes
  • Easy to clean and maintain
  • Fits standard IV/NS bottles

Use Cases

Where Sterile Case Works

  • Hospitals, clinics, nursing homes.
  • Disaster/field hospitals (due to easy setup and mobility)
  • Ambulances (portable and compact usage)
  • Home healthcare setups
  • Emergency wards

Value Proposition

Care that matters

  • Lightweight: Easier for staff to handle
  • Quick Access: Simplifies bottle placement/removal
  • Robust Design: Long-lasting and durable
  • Reusable: Cost-effective for hospitals
